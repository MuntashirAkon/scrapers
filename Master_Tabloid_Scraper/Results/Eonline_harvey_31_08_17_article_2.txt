
Many other celebrities have also used social media to help raise awareness for the plight of the Harvey victims and many have put their own money where their mouth is, including Chris Young, who has donated $100,000 to relief efforts and launched his own crowdfunding initiative. So did Kevin Hart, who has contributed $50,000 himself.In addition, Sandra Bullock has donated $1 million to the American Red Cross to benefit the Harvey victims, while Kim Kardashian announced her family will be donating $500,000 to the organization as well as to the Salvation Army.

To donate to the Red Cross' Hurricane Harvey relief fund, click here.Other organizations—including Coalition for the Homeless, Houston Food Bank, Houston SPCA, Salvation Army and Save the Children—are also accepting donations for relief efforts.
