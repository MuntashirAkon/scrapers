Miami Detective Reveals Herman Echevarria's Cause Of Death/n
Herman Echevarria‘s shocking death was not related to foul play, RadarOnline.com has exclusively learned.
“It was a natural death,” Detective Anthony Reyes revealed to Radar.
Reyes also confirmed that drugs and alcohol were not involved in Echevarria’s sudden passing, and that he was found at the Epic Hotel in Miami, Fla. on September 26.

The coroner’s office told Radar that his body has been released to the Caballero Rivero Woodlawn Funeral Home, where a viewing will be held on September 28. He is expected to be laid to rest at Our Lady of Mercy Catholic Cemetery on September 29.
Echevarria, 61, appeared on The Real Housewives of Miami alongside his then-wife Alexia. The couple separated in 2015, but the 49-year-old released a statement via Instagram shortly after learning of her ex’s passing.





 The Echevarria family is heartbroken over the sudden passing of our patriarch Herman Echevarria this morning. He was a family man, a hardworking businessman, dedicated to helping others and a pillar of his community that was loved by everyone who’s life he touched. We ask that you Please respect our privacy during this time of grieving. – Alexia Echevarria
