*NSYNC’s Chris Kirkpatrick and his wife Karly are expecting their first child together!

The couple officially confirmed the news to People in a statement released on Friday, Mar. 31.
“Karly and I are over the moon excited that we are welcoming a new addition to the family,” Chris told the magazine. “We are both looking forward to becoming parents, and learning how much fun having a child will be.”
