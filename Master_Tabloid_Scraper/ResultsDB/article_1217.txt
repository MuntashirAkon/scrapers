The 27th Annual GLAAD Media Award nominees have been announced!

Best movie of the year Carol is amongst the nominees for Outstanding Film, as well as The Danish Girl, Dope, Freeheld, and Grandma. 
Caitlyn Jenner, who came out as a woman last year, got a nod for her docu-series I Am Cait in the Outstanding Reality Program (plus a TV Journalism nomination for her 20/20 interview with Diane Sawyer), as did Jazz Jennings for her docu-series I Am Jazz.
In television, shows like Orange Is the New Black, Sense8, Orphan Black, Master of None, The Fosters, Transparent and Empire got nods in Outstanding Comedy Series and Outstanding Drama Series categories.

Meanwhile, in the Outstanding Music Category, Miley Cyrus, Adam Lambert, Brandy Carlile, Troye Silvan and Le1f were nominated for their new albums.
The GLAAD Media Awards recognize and honor media for their fair, accurate and inclusive representations of the lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender community and the issues that affect their lives.
This year’s awards show will air on Logo in April. The ceremonies will be held on April 2 at the Beverly Hilton in Los Angeles, and on May 14 at the Waldorf Astoria New York in NYC.
