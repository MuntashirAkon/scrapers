And so continues the advent calendar of awards show nominations.

Angela Bassett, Chloë Grace Moretz, Dennis Quaid and America Ferrera announced the nominees for the 2016 Golden Globes awards on Thursday, Dec. 10. from the Beverly Hilton Hotel. Below is the complete list of contenders, honoring Lady Gaga, Jon Hamm, and Amy Schumer’s Trainwreck.
The 73rd Annual Golden Globe Awards air on Sunday, Jan. 10, 2016 on NBC, hosted by comedian Ricky Gervais.
