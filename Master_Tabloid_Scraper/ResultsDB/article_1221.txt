Did your favorites make the cut?

The nominees for the 22nd annual Screen Actors Guild Awards were announced on Wednesday, Dec. 9. Game of Thrones, Homeland, and House of Cards take the cake with three nominations each, yet fan favorites Empire and Inside Amy Schumer were left with none.
Here is the full list of categories and their nominees. The SAG Awards air on Saturday, Jan. 30 on TNT/TBS.
