Awards season is officially underway with Thursday morning’s announcement of the 2015 Emmy Award nominees.

Cat Deeley (So You Think You Can Dance) and Uzo Aduba (Orange is the New Black) teamed up this morning to announce the shows and actors who will be honored at the 67th Emmy Awards. Deely comes as a last-minute replacement for John Stamos after he checked into rehab to seek help for substance abuse on Friday, July 10.
Did the cast of Mad Men get nominated for an Outstanding Ensemble Musical Performance in a Drama Series? Was Jimmy Fallon’s microsurgeon acknowledged for Excellence in Latenight Cowboy Boots?
Take a deep breath and scroll. Here are your 2015 Emmy Award Nominees.
