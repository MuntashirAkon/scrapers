"Home Alone" Dad John Heard Found Dead, Reportedly At A Hotel/n
Radar has confirmed that actor John Heard, best known for playing the father in Home Alone, has died.
The Medical Examiner’s office in Palo Alto, California, told Radar, “John Heard, age 71, a resident of Studio City, California, died in Palo Alto.” The official said he was not at liberty to reveal any other details at this time.
Palo Alto police said, “We don’t have any information.”
While the medical examiner’s spokesman reported him as 71 when he died, other reports said Heard was 72.

According to reports, the coroner is investigating, and the cause of Heard’s death is not known at this time.
