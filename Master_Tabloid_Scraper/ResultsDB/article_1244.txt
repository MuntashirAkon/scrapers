Awards season is revving up!Less than 24 hours before the 73rd Annual Golden Globe nominations will be revealed at a press conference, nominations for the 22nd Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards were announced live at the Pacific Design Center in L.A. Wednesday.SAG-AFTRA executive vice president Gabrielle Carteris invited Anna Faris and Anthony Mackie, who co-starred in 2011's What's Your Number?, to read the list of nominees in categories spanning film and TV. The event was broadcast live on TNT, TBS and truTV, and streamed via sagawards.tntdrama.com and truTV.com.The 2016 SAG Awards will be simulcast live on TNT and TBS at 8 p.m. ET on Saturday, Jan. 30, 2016. More than 110,000 SAG-AFTRA members selected the 2016 nominees. Final voting begins Wednesday, Dec. 16, and closes Friday, Jan. 29, at 12 p.m. PT. Television icon Carol Burnett will receive the SAG Life Achievement Award.Here is the complete list of nominations:
