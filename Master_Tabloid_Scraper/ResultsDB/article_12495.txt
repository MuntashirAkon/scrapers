Anheuser-Busch Welcomes Adorable New Budweiser Clydesdale Days Before Super Bowl/n
A future Super Bowl star is born! 
On Tuesday, Anheuser-Busch announced a new Budweiser Clydesdale was born. Mac is the first foal to join the family in 2016. The tiny horse is already trotting around on four legs and will eventually grow into one of the giant iconic animals featured in Budweiser’s popular Super Bowl ads.
