Romance and violence seems to be the name of the game for Bonnie & Clyde, judging from the glossy one-minute promo featuring Hirsch and Grainger as the depression-era outlaw couple whose criminal exploits have assured them lasting fame for eight decades. Guns, car crashes, robberies, it's all there!
