Drew Brees Swings Both Ways ... Really Well/nNew Orleans Saints quarterback Drew Brees is known for throwing the long ball -- but he can also hit 'em deep ... from both sides of the plate.It all went down during a charity event in Louisiana called the  "NFL vs. SWAT Softball Showdown " -- which Brees and his fellow Saints barely lost by a score of 18-17 ... despite the Super Bowl MVP smashing 3 homers during the game.Brees dominated the home run derby though -- hitting 'em as a righty and a lefty ... and busting out an awesome moonwalk during his homer trot.Clearly the Madden Curse doesn't apply to softball.../n
