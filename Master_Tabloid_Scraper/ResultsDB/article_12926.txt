Jerry O'Connell Sings Karaoke, Puts Heart and Soul Into The Weeknd Song/nJerry O'Connell put his heart and soul into a karaoke performance ... and the crowd definitely appreciated the effort. The  "Crossing Jordan " and  "Kangaroo Jack " star grabbed the mic at Fraser Tavern in North Bay, Ontario Thursday night and did his best impression of The Weekend's  "I Feel it Coming " -- not holding anything back ... even on the high notes. We're told the place is a local karaoke hot spot, and when the DJ noticed the actor in the crowd he called him up ... and Jerry answered the call.
