Madonna’s twin girls are taking after their mom when it comes to their passion for singing.

Madonna shared a video on Instagram on Saturday (Feb. 18, 2017) of Stella and Esther Mwale, the twin girls that she adopted from Malawi earlier this month, singing the popular song “Twinkle, Twinkle Little Star.” Wearing matching outfits, the enthusiastic sisters are accompanied by a piano player in the background.
