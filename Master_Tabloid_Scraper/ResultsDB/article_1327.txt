Angelina Jolie Signs Up For New Flick!/n

After wrapping up work on her writing/directorial debut In the Land of Blood and Honey, Angelina Jolie has found a new film in which to star, and it sounds as though it's going to be fantastic!
The actress will be reportedly be taking the title role in Gertrude Bell, which is a biopic about the woman responsible for establishing the modern framework for Jordan and Iraq!
Ridley Scott's Scott Free Productions is currently developing the project, written by Jeffrey Caine, and the famed director is even apparently thinking about taking the helm on it himself!
Sounds like some fascinating subject matter - and we couldn't think of anyone more perfect for it than Angie!
