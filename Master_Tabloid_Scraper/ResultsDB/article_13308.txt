Bachelor Nick Viall and Vanessa Grimaldi have called off their engagement, more than five months after they announced their love on the hit reality show, RadarOnline.com has learned.

We will continue to be there for each other no matter what. This hasn’t been an easy decision, however, as we part ways, we do so with lots of love and admiration for each other.”
