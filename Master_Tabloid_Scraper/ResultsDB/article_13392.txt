'Bachelorette' Star Eric Bigger Ain't Broken Up Over Rachel Lindsay's Rejection /nEric Bigger is the exact opposite of heartbroken after getting rejected by Rachel Lindsay on the finale of  "The Bachelorette " ... in fact, he almost seems a little too happy.
