Ben Stiller's Mom Dies -- Anne Meara Dead at 85/nActress and comedian Anne Meara -- who famously co stared with her husband Jerry Stiller for more than 60 years -- has died at the age of 85. Anne's family, including her son Ben Stiller released a statement on Sunday regarding her passing. They did not mention a cause of death.  Meara was famous for performing on  "The Ed Sullivan Show " in the 1960s with her husband Jerry Stiller, and also had recurring roles on  "All My Children " and  "The King of Queens. " She co stared with Ben in the 2006 film 'Night at the Museum. " Anne's death was first reported by the AP./n
