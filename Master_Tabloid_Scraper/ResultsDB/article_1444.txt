Tori Spelling & Dean McDermott Welcome Baby No. 5/n
Tori Spelling and Dean McDermott welcomed their fifth child together on Thursday, March 2, RadarOnline.com has learned.
According to PEOPLE magazine, their son, Beau Dean McDermott, was born at 1:48 p.m., weighing 5 lbs., 12 oz.
The little boy is the latest addition to the couple’s brood of five, including son Liam, 9, and daughters Stella, 8, Hattie, 5, and Finn, 5.
“We are over the moon in love with baby Beau. He is a true blessing and his brothers and sisters were overjoyed to meet him! We are all truly grateful for our big beautiful and healthy family,” Spelling, 43, told PEOPLE in a statement.
