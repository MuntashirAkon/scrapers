Lela Rose Partners With Beauty.com for Exclusive Clutch/n

Lela Rose is partnering with Beauty.com just in time for summer! The designer has created a vibrant clutch in a fun, Peruvian-inspired print from her spring 2011 collection. Customers who visit beauty.com can get the exclusive bag with the purchase of $100 or more on the site. The bag, valued at $140, comes filled with 14 deluxe beauty samples like sunscreen and self-tanner, plus a $10 Beauty.com shopping card for future purchases. Get yours at beauty.com.
