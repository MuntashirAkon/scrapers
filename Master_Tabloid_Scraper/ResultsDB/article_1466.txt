Ariana Grande & Nicki Minaj Are Going To Get The Crowd Moving Side To Side At The 2016 VMAS — Because They're Performing Together!/n

These dangerous women are teaming up once again!
On Wednesday, MTV announced Ariana Grande and Nicki Minaj will be joining forces on the VMAs stage at Madison Square Garden!
Related: Britney Spears Will Be Performing, Too!
The dynamic duo — who previously teamed up on the hit track Bang Bang with Jessie J — will be performing their cool collab Side To Side, off the pint-sized pop princess' third album Dangerous Woman.
The fierce females aren't the only A-listers slated to rock the stage during Sunday's ceremony!
The gorgeous gals join Britney Spears, G-Eazy, Nick Jonas, Ty Dolla $ign, Future, and Rihanna as performers in the star-studded lineup!
