'Star Wars' Actor -- Dead at 95/nJason Wingreen, who voiced the villain Boba Fett in  "Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back " and played Harry the bartender on  "All In The Family " has died.  Wingreen died at his home in L.A. on Christmas day according to a statement from his son.  Wingreen, who has almost 200 credits to his name also appeared on  "The Twlight Zone, "  "Archie Bunker's Place " and  "The Untouchables. " The Hollywood Reporter, which broke the story, said he died after a brief illness.  He was 95-years-old. R.I.P./n
