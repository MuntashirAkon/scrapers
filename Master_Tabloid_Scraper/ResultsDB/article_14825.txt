Jean Stapleton Dead -- 'All in the Family' Star Dies at 90/nJean Stapleton -- who played the iconic role of Edith Bunker on  "All in the Family " died Friday in New York City. She was 90.  According to her family, Stapleton passed away of natural causes.Stapleton won three Emmys from her work on  "All in the Family " opposite Carroll O’Connor’s equally iconic character, Archie Bunker.Stapleton's last major role was in the 1998 film  "You've Got Mail. " She played a co-worker of Meg Ryan's character.Her husband of 26 years, William Putch, passed away in 1983. She is survived by her two children, John and Pamela./n
