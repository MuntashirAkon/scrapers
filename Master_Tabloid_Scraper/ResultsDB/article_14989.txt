Todd Chrisley: Sara Evans Showing Me the Ropes for Live Country Debut/nTodd Chrisley's not even done recording his first country album yet, but he's already jumping on a stage to perform in Nashville. The  "Chrisley Knows Best " patriarch was in the studio Thursday with country singer Sara Evans, producer Nash Overstreet and songwriter Shane Stevens. We're told Chrisley and Evans recorded a duet -- no word on the title, but they're planning to test Todd's pipes with an upcoming live performance. He'll have old pro Sara onstage with him, but it could be his make or break moment. Stay tuned./n
