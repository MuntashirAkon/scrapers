Kelly Clarkson, Kristen Bell, Ben Stiller & More Celebs Sing Hamilton Songs For Lin-Manuel Miranda's #Ham4All Challenge!/n

Celebrities are showing up for a good cause!
As we reported, Lin-Manuel Miranda launched the #Ham4All contest, which gives fans an opportunity to win tickets to the famed musical's El Lay debut if they share snippets of them singing Hamilton songs and donate $10 to the Hispanic Federation's immigrants rights coalition.
In response to the viral video challenge, Kelly Clarkson, Kristen Bell, Ben Stiller, and so many other celebs have taken on the feat!
