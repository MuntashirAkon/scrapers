RHOC Star Tamra Judge Reunites With Estranged Daughter Sidney At High School Graduation/n

We're glad Tamra Judge's family is putting aside their differences!
On Wednesday, the Real Housewives of Orange County star revealed on Instagram that she reunited with estranged daughter Sidney Barney at the 18-year-old's high school graduation.

The reality TV personality posted: 




So many things to be proud of this month. Our family has come a long way and I couldn't be happier. Simon and I are so proud of these amazing, smart, funny,beautiful & crazy kids. Congrats Sidney ❤️ your smile lightens up the room and your future is so bright. #gaveituptojesus #2017 👨‍🎓 #familyA post shared by Tamra Judge (@tamrajudge) on Jun 14, 2017 at 12:52pm PDT


The 49-year-old has three children with ex Simon Barney — including Spencer (17) and Sophia — and has oldest son Ryan Vieth from a previous relationship. She is currently married to Eddie Judge.
On the season 10 reunion back in 2015, Miz Judge revealed Sidney was living with her father, and the two hadn't seen each other in two years.
