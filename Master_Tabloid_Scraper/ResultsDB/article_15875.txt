‘Boyhood’, ‘Birdman’ Win Big at the 2015 Critics’ Choice Awards/n





                    2014
                







12 Years a Slave wins big at Critics Choice Awards

 MORE >>





 Awards season continued on at the 20th annual Critics Choice Awards, held in Los Angeles last night (Jan. 15).
Birdman led the pack , winning 6 of its 13 nominations. Boyhood also came out on top winning Best Picture, and taking 4 out of its 8 nods, finally with The Grand Budapest Hotel winning 3 out of its 11 nominations.
