Happy birthday, Betty White!The Golden Girls and Hot in Cleveland alum turned 94 on Sunday and is as busy and active as ever; The actress appears with Rob Riggle and Joel McHale in a new ad for Bandwagon.net, during which she absolutely steals the show.The commercial, which debuted during FOX's NFL Sunday pregame show's "Riggle's Picks" segment, before the Seattle Seahawks-Carolina Panthers game, sees White chatting on the phone with NFL personalities. A Star Wars Stormtrooper also makes an appearance.
