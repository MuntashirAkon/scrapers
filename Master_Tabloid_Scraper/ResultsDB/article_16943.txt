Mike Myers and His Wife File for Divorce/nBy THE ASSOCIATED PRESS LOS ANGELES -- Actor-comedian Mike Myers and his wife, Robin Ruzan, are ending their 12-year marriage, a spokeswoman said Friday.   "They remain committed and caring friends, " publicist Ina Treciokas said in a statement.  Myers and Ruzan, a writer and actress, were married in May 1993. They have no children.  Myers, formerly of 'Saturday Night Live,' has starred in all three 'Austin Powers' films and voiced the giant green ogre in the 'Shrek' films./n
