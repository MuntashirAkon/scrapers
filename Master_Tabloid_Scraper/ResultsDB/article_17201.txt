The coroner's report says,  "no illegal narcotics were involved in her death " ... and she was stage 4. Erin's huband, Steve Fleischmann, had written an open letter describing Erin's months-long battle with squamous cell carcinoma. The  "Happy Days " and  "Joanie Loves Chachi " star died last month, at the age of 56. Steve and Erin were together for 25 years. He says she died holding his hand./n
