The Walking Dead wants you to know one thing about season seven: Nobody is safe. Gulp.The new key art for season seven features new cast addition Jeffrey Dean Morgan as Negan swinging his trusty sidekick Lucille. Yes, Lucille is the name of his bat covered in barbed wire. Viewers will remember (how could you forget?) that season six of The Walking Dead ended with Negan murdering one of your favorite zombie apocalypse survivors. Their identity has been kept a secret until Sunday, Oct. 23 at 9 p.m. on AMC.
