Jared Padalecki Welcomes Second Baby. It’s a Boy!/n
Last night, Jared Padalecki took to Twitter to announce that he and his wife Genevieve Cortese Padalecki welcomed their second son on Sunday.

Ok. For real ;) My amazingly strong wife home-birthed a happy and healthy baby boy last night :). Please send love :))
— Jared Padalecki (@jarpad) December 24, 2013
 
The couple have yet to share any further information about the birth, including the son’s name.
Padalecki and Cortese Padalecki, who met on Supernatural, wed in February 2010 and are already parents to Thomas Colton, 21 months.
