5 Seconds of Summer -- Band Member Torched During Concert (VIDEO)/nMichael Clifford, guitarist for the band 5 Seconds of Summer, was set on fire Saturday night when a fireball engulfed him on stage. The London concert included pyrotechnics where fire would shoot from portions of the stage.  Michael was standing too close when a ball of flames lit his hair on fire, badly burning his face. The show had almost ended -- the band was about to do an encore - but the show abruptly ended and Michael was taken to the hospital. Michael tweeted that his condition is stable, adding,  "What the f***s up.  sorry I couldnt finish the encore but at least I look like two face, thanks for worrying.
