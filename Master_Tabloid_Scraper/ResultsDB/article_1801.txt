Wrestling Legend Fabulous Moolah Dies/nThe most famous and recognized woman wrestler, Fabulous Moolah, passed away last night in South Carolina, the WWE announced today. She was 84. Born Lillian Ellison, the WWE says that she  "was the longest reigning champion in the history of her chosen sport, or any sport for that matter. " She even held the title as recently as 1999, becoming the oldest champion in the history of wrestling.She was inducted into the WWF Hall of Fame back in 1995, the first female wrestler to receive the honor./n
