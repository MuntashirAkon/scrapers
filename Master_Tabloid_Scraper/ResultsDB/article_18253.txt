Eunice Shriver: Recovering/n
Seriously ill Eunice Kennedy Shriver, 79 (sister of John, Robert and  Teddy Kennedy), is expected to make a full recovery from a postoperative infection, her son, Mark Shriver, told reporters on Monday. The elder Shiver, who had a benign pancreatic tumor removed on Oct. 12 at John Hopkins Hospital in Baltimore, was later released from the hospital only to be readmitted 11 days later. The mother of Maria Shriver, she is best known for having formed the Special Olympics in 1968, in memory of her autistic sister, Rosemary Kennedy.
