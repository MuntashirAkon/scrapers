 It’s almost awards season, folks.
To kick things off, the fine people at the Screen Actors Guild have just announced nominees for the 21st annual fete.
Ready?
Birdman leads the way with four nominations, including Michael Keaton for Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Leading Role and Emma Stone for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Supporting Role.
Right behind Keaton and company is Richard Linklater’s wonderful Boyhood with three nominations. The film is also up for the Best Cast prize.
