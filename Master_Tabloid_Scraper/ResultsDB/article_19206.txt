Jackson Body In Berry Gordy’s Crypt /n
The mystery is over.
The body of Michael Jackson rests in Motown Records’ founder Berry Gordy’s Crypt at Forest Lawn Memorial Park in Hollywood Hills, CA, RadarOnline.com has learned.
Sources tell us it’s a temporary resting place while the Jackson family find a permanent resting place.
There was much confusion as to where Jackson’s body was taken, if anywhere, following the massive public memorial held at the Staples Center on Tuesday.
The Gordy crypt, which is described as a sparse, empty mausoleum highlighted by a G monogram and located on the same property as the graves for such Hollywood legends Lucille Ball, Bette Davis, Gene Autry, comic Freddie Prinze and flamboyant entertainer Liberace. /n
