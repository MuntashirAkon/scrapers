Kid-Free Zone! 15 Hollywood Stars Who Don’t Want Children/n
They have some of the best genes on the planet so it’s sad to think that some of the most gorgeous people in the world don’t want to procreate. But while some celebs refuse to be pressured into having kids (think Jennifer Aniston), some have openly said they don’t want children at all. Here are a few stars who don’t want to hear the pitter-patter of tiny feet.
