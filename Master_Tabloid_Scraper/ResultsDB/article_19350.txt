The British are coming! No, wait—it’s just their nominations.

The 2016 BAFTA nominees were announced on Friday, Jan. 8, with Carol and Bridge of Spies leading the pack with nine nominations a piece. Alicia Vikander pulled in the most individual nominations with a whopping total of two, surrounded by her outstanding actor peers which include Eddie Redmayne and Saoirse Ronan.
The winners will be announced on Feb. 14, 2016 in London in a broadcast hosted by BBC One. Launch the gallery at the top of the page to view photos of the best dressed actors from last year’s BAFTA Awards.
