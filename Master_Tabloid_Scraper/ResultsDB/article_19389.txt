Prince Philip has been hospitalized due to a nasty infection, RadarOnline.com can report.
“The Duke of Edinburgh was admitted to King Edward VII Hospital in London last night, as a precautionary measure, for treatment of an infection arising from a pre-existing condition,” said a palace spokesman in statement released this morning to PEOPLE.
Buckingham Palace announced that the 96-year-old husband of Queen Elizabeth is ill yet in “good spirits” and “disappointed to be missing the State Opening of Parliament and Royal Ascot.”
