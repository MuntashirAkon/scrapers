Queen Is Going on Tour with Adam Lambert/n
Music legend Queen is teaming up with Adam Lambert once again, embarking on a 25-city arena tour this summer.

Kicking off in June, the North American tour will make stops in Los Angeles, Las Vegas, Dallas, Boston, and New York City. There will also be several shows in Canadian cities, including Toronto, Vancouver and Montreal.
PressRoomVIP reports:
Tickets for the tour will go on sale Friday, February 3rd at 10 a.m. local time. If you happen to be a part of Queen or Adam’s fan clubs, you get early bird beginning Tuesday, January 31st at 10 a.m.
