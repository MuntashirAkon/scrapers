Alice Cooper -- Tiger Woods Is No Loser ... He'll Win 10 More Tourneys! /nNot everyone has given up on Tiger Woods ... in fact, famed golf-aholic Alice Cooper says Tiger is still a BEAST -- and will win at least 10 more tournaments before he hangs 'em up. As we previously reported, Cooper is one of the most well-respected celebrity golfers on the circuit -- and even considered turning pro a few years back. Translation -- the guy knows what he's talking about. And in NYC Tuesday, Cooper was adamant that Woods is not as washed up as some people make him out to be.  "If you ask the young guys ... they'll probably tell you he's still the BEST golfer, " Cooper says. For the record, Tiger is currently ranked #43 in the world./n
