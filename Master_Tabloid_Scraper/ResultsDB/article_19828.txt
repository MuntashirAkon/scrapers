[VIDEO]/nFor a few minutes at a small high school in the middle of California, Michael Jackson was resurrected ... in the form of a skinny, redheaded white teenager.It all went down last Friday at Pitman High School in Turlock, CA ... located near Modesto. 17-year-old Brett Nichols stole the show with his MJ routine ... set of course to  "Billie Jean. " We're told 60 different acts auditioned to take part in the show, but only nine got to actually perform for the school.
