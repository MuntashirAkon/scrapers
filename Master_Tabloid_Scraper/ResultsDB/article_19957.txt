Well, here's is a match made in fashion heaven.H&M's next designer collaboration will be with Isabel Marant. The collection will feature clothing and accessories for both men and women and hits stores on Nov. 14.

"I aim at creating something real, that women want to wear in their everyday lives, with a certain carelessness, which I think is very Parisian: you dress up but do not pay too much attention and still look sexy," the designer said in a release. "The collection is infused with this kind of easiness and attitude."

H&M has a exciting history of big name partnerships with designers such as Karl Lagerfeld, Lanvin, and Versace.
