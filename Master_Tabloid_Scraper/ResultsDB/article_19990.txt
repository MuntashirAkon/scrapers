Not every star got it right on the fashion front  this week!
From the red carpet of the most stylish soirees and awards shows to the city streets, Hollywood’s hottest celebrities were dressed to rock and shock in the best, worst and wackiest fashions – and RadarOnline.com has all the wardrobe winners and style sinners in our weekly feature.
PHOTOS: The Best, Worst & Wackiest Dressed Stars Of The Week
It’s the final few weeks before the Academy Awards and things are heating up with the hottest nominees! At The Hollywood Reporter Nominees Night 2013, Jennifer Lawrence  rocked a sexy Valentino cocktail dress, and Jessica Chastain donned a gorgeous Givenchy peplum number. Naomi Watts hit the 11th Annual Visual Effects Society Awards, looking red hot in a Zac Posen strapless, while Anne Hathaway  looked pretty in Prada pink for the Paris premiere of Les Miserables.
Sarah Jessica Parker’s  amfAR Gala fashion was a bit off, drenching her figure in a very colorful Maison Martin Margiela Artisanal gown, while Heidi Klum steamed things up in Michael Kors. Lindsay Lohan also made a surprise appearance at the event, committing a fashion fur pas with her THEIA GOWN.
Safe Haven beauty Julianne Hough may have killed it at her big Los Angeles premiere  in a skinsational Alberta Ferretti gown, blinged out with Martin Katz jewels, but she also had a fashion flop at the Sole Society preview party in Los Angeles, wearing a fringed white ensemble with red heels.
