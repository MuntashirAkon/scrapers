Jay-Z is going on the road!

The rapper has announced the North American leg of his tour in support of his new album, 4:44. Kicking off this fall in Anaheim, Calif., the 31-show trek will also include performances in Las Vegas, Miami, Chicago, Toronto, Vancouver, and — of course — Jay’s hometown of Brooklyn.
