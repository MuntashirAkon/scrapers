Iman Addresses David Bowie’s Death at ‘First Night Out’ Since His Passing/n
Iman attended her first event since the death of her husband, David Bowie, in January. The supermodel has stayed out of the public eye since her longtime love passed away after a battle with liver cancer at age 69.

The HSN fashion designer, 61, stepped out for Tom Ford’s New York Fashion Week show on Wednesday, September 7, and opened up to Good Morning America’s Joe Zee about losing her husband and then her mother, Marian Abdulmajid, less than three months later. “It’s been a tough year, but I’m holding up,” she said.
