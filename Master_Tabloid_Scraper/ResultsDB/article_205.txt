Paula Deen -- Our Maid Robbed Us Blind/nFood Network chef Paula Deen says her maid cleaned her out of more than $10,000 in jewelry and other expensive stuff before she fired her and had her arrested.According to a police report filed by Paula's husband, Michael Groover, and obtained by TMZ, Mary Alice White stole jewelry and other precious metals during a fairly long period of time -- August, 2009 to May, 2010. Groover claims the maid did not have a sex bias ...  she stole men's and women's jewelry -- allegedly.Groover told cops when he found some of the missing jewelry in White's possession, he and Paula fired her immediately.  Funny the way that happens ...White was arrested on May 5 and arraigned on charges of felony theft./n
