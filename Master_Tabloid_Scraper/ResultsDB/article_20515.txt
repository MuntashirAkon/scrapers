By Leah Ornstein – Radar Features Editor
Who sported winning and sinning style this week?
From the red carpet of the hottest events to the city streets, Hollywood’s hottest stars were dressed to rock and shock in the best, worst & wackiest fashions – and RadarOnline.com has all the photos in our weekly feature!
PHOTOS: The Best, Worst & Wackiest Dressed Stars Of The Week
That was fast! Mother of three Jennifer Garner returned to the red carpet in smokin’ hot mama style this week at the Los Angeles premiere of The Odd Life of Timothy Green. Mrs. Ben Affleck returned showed off her already slimmed-down figure in a divinely draped Lanvin number paired with glittery grey sandals and Neil Lane bling, just six months after giving birth to her first boy.
Another hot mama, Pink, also rocked the red carpet as she was announced as the latest face of Covergirl! Showing off her fab abs in a cutout Norma Kamali dress paired with Alexander McQueen heels, the sassy singer scored one of best looks ever.
That’s what you call a “fresh airport look?” Kim Kardashian was the biggest a la mode miss of the week, sharing her flying fashion faux pas on Twitter, which included leather Celine sweatpants and Kanye West for Giuseppe Zanotti heels. The day before, Kim hit a Los Angeles nail salon, makeup free and wearing black workout pants without undergarments.
As for wacky, Lady Gaga grabbed attention in putrid pink getup, shopping at Giorgio Armani wearing the femininely hued color from head to toe.
