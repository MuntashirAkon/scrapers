It’s back!

The full lineup for the 24th season of Dancing with the Stars has been announced. This year, we’ll see a Bachelor, two Olympians, a Fifth Harmony member, a handful of TV actors, a Real Housewives star, and several athletes battle it out on the dance floor for the famed Mirror Ball trophy.
Season 24’s contestants and their professional dance partners are:

Glee actress Heather Morris and Maksim Chmerkovskiy
Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Erika Jayne and Gleb Savchenko
Olympic gymnast Simone Biles and Sasha Farber
Fifth Harmony singer Normani Kordei and Valentin Chmerkovskiy
The Bachelor star Nick Viall and Peta Murgatroyd
Former Olympic figure skater Nancy Kerrigan and Artem Chigvintsev
The A-Team actor Mr. T and Kym Johnson-Herjavec
’70s TV icon Charo and Keo Motsepe
Saturday Night Live alum Chris Kattan and Witney Carson
Bull rider Bonner Bolton and Sharna Burgess
Retired Chicago Cubs catcher David Ross and Lindsay Arnold
Football player Rashad Jennings and Emma Slater

 
The show returns to ABC on March 20. For now, see the cast’s official portraits — below!
