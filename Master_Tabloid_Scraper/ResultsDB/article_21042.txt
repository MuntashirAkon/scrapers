Miracle on 34th Street Actress Maureen O'Hara Has Died/nMaureen O'Hara, an actress who appeared in legendary movies like  "Miracle on 34th Street " and  "The Quiet Man " has died. She was 95 years old. A family spokesperson says O'Hara died of natural causes at her home in Idaho surrounded by her loved ones on Saturday. The statement says her family played music from her favorite movie,  "The Quiet Man " during her final hours.  O'Hara -- originally from Ireland -- played Natalie Woods' mother, Doris Walker in  "Miracle " in 1947. She also starred in several movies with John Wayne. O'Hara received an honorary Oscar last year. /n
