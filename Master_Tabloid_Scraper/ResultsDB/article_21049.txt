Doris Roberts Died of a Stroke/nDoris Roberts died from a massive stroke ... according to her death certificate. TMZ obtained a copy of the doc, filed in L.A. County Court -- and it reveals the  "Everybody Loves Raymond " star had been suffering from hypertension for years. The immediate cause of death though ... was a  "large hemorrhagic cerebrovascular accident " ... the medical term for a stroke. Doris died at home last month. She was 90 years old./n
