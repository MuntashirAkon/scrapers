Tom Hanks To Reunite With Longtime Buddy At TV Land Awards/n
Tom Hanks and Peter Scolari reunite as the honorees at the 8th Annual TV Land Awards, the network said Monday.
The network will salute the 30th anniversary of the ground-breaking sitcom Bosom Buddies with the Anniversary Award at this year’s show.
The show’s other cast members — Donna Dixon, Thelma Hopkins and Holland Taylor — will also be in attendance, marking the first time that the cast has been together publicly since the show went off the air.
