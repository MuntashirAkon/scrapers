This Star Wasn't Born -- She Was Stuffed/nShe claims to have  "the determination of the Energizer bunny, the cool edge of Hello Kitty, the ego of Miss Piggy, and the wardrobe of Barbie. " She hangs out with the likes of Robert Downey Jr., Beyonce and Mary Louise Parker. She's Star Von Bunny -- a stuffed white rabbit. The hysterical new book by Kym Canter and illustrator Ellen Kahn tracks Star's meteoric rise from the rabbit hole to stardom, dishing out pearls of wisdom along the way, like,  "Turn your minuses into pluses. I am the only model with a tail. How special is that? " "Star Von Bunny: A Model Tale " is in stores November 27./n
