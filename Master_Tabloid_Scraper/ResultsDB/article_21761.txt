Last night, actress Hilary Duff hosted an exclusive event in New York City for Stella Artois to kick off their “Host One to Remember” summer campaign.
Hilary shared some tips on summer hosting, suggesting to keep the menu simple and flavorful. Her favorite menu items include trays of colorful roasted vegetables, grilled fruit, her honey garlic chicken recipe, and her bacon wrapped dates stuffed with cheese.
The always stylish host wore a Veronica Beard midi dress, Jeffrey Levinson clutch, Cartier bracelet, and Steve Madden heels to the evening at Chez Sebastian garden.
