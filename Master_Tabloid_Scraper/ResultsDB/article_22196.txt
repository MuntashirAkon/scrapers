Bruce Beresford-Redman Shows Up to Court/nBruce Beresford-Redman just showed up to court in downtown Los Angeles, where a hearing is taking place over his murdered wife's estate.As we reported earlier this month, Bruce's father David filed legal papers to validate Monica Burgos Beresford-Redman's 2008 will  -- which names Bruce as the executor of her estate.The will also gives David and his wife Juanita interest in a house Monica owned -- while the rest of her property goes to Bruce. Monica's sisters have said they will challenge the validity of the will.Stay tuned.../n
