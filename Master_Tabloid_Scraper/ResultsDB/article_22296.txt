Firefighters Surprise Woman Whose Late Husband Was Fireman in Same Department on Her 100th Birthday/n
Jeanette Carty received the surprise of a lifetime on her 100th birthday.   
On June 22, the longtime Norfolk, Virginia, resident was greeted on her front porch by dozens of firefighters from Norfolk Fire-Rescue holding flowers and wishing her a happy birthday.   
Norfolk Fire-Rescue says the department has always been a part of Carty’s life. Her late husband, Joe, “served the city for 30 years, starting in 1942; as well as her nephew, Capt. David bear and his father,” according to the Fire-Rescue’s Facebook page.   
Photos capturing the heartwarming moment were posted to social media on June 23.   
Carty is pictured grinning from ear to ear she receives a sunflower bouquet from “Battalion 3, Engine 11, Recruit class 155 and a few members from training.”
