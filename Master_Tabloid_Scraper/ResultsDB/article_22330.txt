 Just because you haven’t heard of medieval peasants wielding gatling guns doesn’t mean they didn’t exist – according to Hansel & Gretel: Witch Hunters, anyway.
In the trailer for the upcoming action film, Jeremy Renner and his on-screen sibling Gemma Arterton collect an impressive amount of artillery as they hunt down witches as part of a lifelong vendetta against supernatural creatures.
Which witch is the worst one Hansel and Gretel face?

After a traumatic incident as children, the pair dedicate their lives to dispatching evil, using any- and everything at their disposal, including stakes, swords, and evidently, numerous mechanical devices that fire dozens of bullets in rapid succession.
