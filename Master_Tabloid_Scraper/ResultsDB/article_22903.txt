'Deadliest Catch' Guy Suffers Stroke/nPhil Harris -- captain of the Cornelia Marie on  "Deadliest Catch " -- suffered a stroke and is currently hospitalized, a Discovery rep tells us. A spokesman for the show tells TMZ,  "Phil Harris suffered a stroke while in port off-loading from the F /V Cornelia Marie. He has been transported to a medical facility and is receiving the best care possible. Our thoughts and prayers are with his family and we will make more information available as we know it. "Phil has been captain of the Cornelia Marie for 18 years, according to the Discovery website, where his two sons serve as deckhands./n
