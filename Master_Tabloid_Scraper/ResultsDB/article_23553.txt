NFL Clears James Harrison, Clay Matthews & Julius Peppers In PED Case/nThat noise? James Harrison, Clay Matthews and Julius Peppers all breathing a sigh of relief ... 'cause the NFL just announced the players have been cleared by the leagues PED investigation. The investigation stemmed from allegations made in a documentary by Al Jazeera America ... where the news outlet claimed the NFL players were linked to PED use. The NFL conducted interviews with all 3 players last week ... and announced Wednesday that they found  "no credible evidence " that the guys were  "provided with or used substances prohibited " under the leagues drug policy. Long story short, back to football./n
