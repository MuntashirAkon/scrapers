'Honeymooners' Alice ... Shiela MacRae Dead/nSheila MacRae -- who played Alice Kramden on  "The Honeymooners, " has died.  MacRae did not appear in the original episodes in 1955.  She appeared on the wildly popular  "Jackie Gleason Show " from 1966 - 1970.  The show recreated  "The Honeymooners " in featured segments.The original Alice was played by Audrey Meadows.  She died in 1996.As for MacRae ... she reportedly died in an actor's nursing home in New Jersey.  Sheila was 93 years old. /n
