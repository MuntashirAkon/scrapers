Picks and Pans Review: Buddy Jewell/n
Times Like TheseCOUNTRY
Jewell, winner of USA Network’s Nashville Star in 2003, rarely rises above the routine on his latest, but he doesn’t embarrass himself either. The singer possesses a warm voice and easy, avuncular style that is best on such straight-ahead country tunes as “If She Were Any Other Woman.” But songs that require some subtlety and sense of humor, like “You Ain’t Doin’ It Right,” seem beyond him.
