Drake and The Chainsmokers are leading the 2017 Billboard Music Awards nominations, tying for the all-time record for most nods in a single year with 22 each.

Twenty One Pilots comes in as the second act with the most nominations as Rihanna follows closely behind with 14 mods of her own, including Top Artist, Top Female Artist, Top Hot 100 Artist, Top Billboard 200 Album and a double nomination for Top R&B Song. Returning winners who also received nominations this year include Adele, Justin Bieber, The Weeknd, Luke Bryan, Enrique Iglesias, Wiz Khalifa and Major Lazer.
This year’s ceremony will take place at T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas on Sunday, May 21 at 8 p.m. ET on ABC.
