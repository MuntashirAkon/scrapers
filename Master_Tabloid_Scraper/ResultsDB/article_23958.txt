Britney Spears' Stylist Testifies/n
     (Photo: Splash Photo Agency) 
Britney Spears hairstylist testified today in court that Brits former manager, Sam Lutfi was texting her non-stop from December 24-28, 2008. With each new text, according to hairstylist Roberta Romero, Britney became more and more frightened. Britney’s former bodyguard also testified that the pop star became so alarmed that she informed her security team of Lutfi’s harassing and frightening text messages. 
At the close of the day, LA Judge Aviva Bobb left the (TRO) temporary restraining orders in place until the next court date, April 1, 2009.
