Ashley Monroe and John Danks Welcome Son Dalton William/n
Ashley Monroe is a mom!
The country singer-songwriter gave birth to a baby boy, her first child with pro baseball player John Danks, on Friday, July 28, her rep confirms to PEOPLE.
Son Dalton William Danks weighed in at 8 lbs. and measures 20 inches long. “He is perfect and his parents are beyond happy,” the rep tells PEOPLE.
“Welcome to the world, love of my life. Dalton William Danks. ❤️,” she captioned an adorable photo series on her social media accounts.

The Pistol Annies singer, 30, wed the former Chicago White Sox player in October 2013. Monroe’s bandmate and best friend Miranda Lambert served as maid of honor, while Lambert’s then-husband Blake Shelton officiated the Tennessee-based ceremony.
