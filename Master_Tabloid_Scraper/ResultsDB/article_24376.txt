Beyonce Salutes Sterling and Castile in Concert /nBeyonce stopped her concert in Scotland for a moment of silence in honor of the most recent alleged victims of police brutality ... before launching into her song,  "Freedom. " She told the crowd in Glasgow,  "Please join me in a moment of silence to acknowledge the lives of Alton Sterling and Philando Castile.
