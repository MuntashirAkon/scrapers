WWE Star Kane Running For Mayor In Tennessee/nThere could be a Big Red Mayor in Tennessee next year ... 'cause WWE legend Kane is running for office!! The 7-foot, 323-pound superstar is prepping to chokeslam the political game in his local Knox County, according to WCYB in Tennessee. Kane -- AKA Glenn Jacobs -- is a Republican and filed paperwork earlier this month, kick-starting the campaign process. The election won't take place until May 2018. As a member of The Authority, Kane was voted Most Hated Wrestler of the Year by Pro Wrestling Illustrated in 2013. Better hope those voters don't live in Knox County./n
