'Walking Dead' Star -- The Show Isn't Racist ... It's the Opposite! (VIDEO)/nSonequa Martin-Green -- one of the few African-Americans on  "The Walking Dead " who hasn't met a swift death -- says the show isn't racist ... it's just coincidence lots of black people die. Sonequa goes a lot further, and says the dye is cast before the actors are chosen, and it's just the luck of the draw that black actors excel in the auditions. Just don't kill Glenn, we beg of you! /n
