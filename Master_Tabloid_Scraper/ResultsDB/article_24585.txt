On Wednesday, the star showed off her massive engagement ring while working the red carpet at the premiere of her Hallmark movie Remembering Sunday in Los Angeles.

Although the ring was clearly her blowout fashion statement of the night, the actress didn't look too shabby, either, as she rocked the event dressed in a sharp two-tone cutout dress and black pumps.Bledel and Kartheiser, 33, made their debut as a couple back in October at the opening of a photography exhibit in New York City. This will be the first marriage for both.
