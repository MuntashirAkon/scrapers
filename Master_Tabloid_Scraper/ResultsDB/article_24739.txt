FC Barcelona, Messi Have Moment of Silence for Terror Victims/nThe city of Barcelona -- and all of its star athletes -- took a moment of silence to honor those injured and killed in Thursday's terror attack.  FC Barcelona stopped Thursday's practice, and stars like Lionel Messi and Gerard Pique stood quietly to reflect. The team will also be wearing armbands during their next game to honor the victims.  Cristiano Ronaldo and his Real Madrid teammates also held a moment of silence during their training session.  At least 14 died when a terrorists plowed a van through a crowded tourist area in Barcelona on Thursday. One hundred people were injured. Cops have made multiple arrests and killed several suspects. They're still hunting for more./n
