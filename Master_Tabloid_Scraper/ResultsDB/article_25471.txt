Miranda Lambert Delivers Emotional 'Tin Man' Performance At ACM Awards/n
Miranda Lambert delivered an emotional performance of her hit song “Tin Man” at the Academy of Country Music Awards in Las Vegas on Sunday, from her album The Weight of These Wings.
Donning a fringed black dress, the 33-year-old performed a stripped down version of the track, singing along with her acoustic guitar.
