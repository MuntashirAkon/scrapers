Monday nights are about to get much more glamorous…and expensive!
The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills’ Erika Jayne Girardi and Glee alum Heather Morris will be joining the cast of Dancing With the Stars this season, RadarOnline.com can exclusively reveal.
The reality star/singer and former Beyonce backup dancer join Bachelor Nick Viall, Fifth Harmony’s Normani Kordei, Olympic gold medalist Simone Biles, actress Charo, comedian Chris Kattan, and Mr. T.
