The Weeknd is heading to this years Grammy Awards as one of its performers. Though his girlfriend, Selena Gomez, isn’t nominated for anything at this year’s ceremony, word has it that the “Starboy” singer is trying his hardest to snag her a ticket.

According to TMZ, the couple, who were last spotted looking oh-so loved up in Italy, have conformed to organizers that they’ll be attending Clive Davis’ annual pre-Grammys party that weekend. The publication claims The Weeknd has also put in a request to The Recording Academy to have Gomez be listed as his date, ensuring that she’ll be seated next to him at the star-studded event.
The Weeknd is slated to hit the stage with Daft Punk at the 2017 show. Adele, Alicia Keys, A Tribe Called Quest, Dave Grohl, country singer Maren Morris and rapper Anderson .Paak will also perform.
The telecast is set to air live on CBS on Sunday, Feb. 12 at 8 p.m. ET/5 p.m. PT.
