There were so many things Lin-Manuel Miranda hadn't done during his time in Hamilton...Like get a haircut. The 36-year-old Tony-winning creator and star of the smash hit Broadway musical has swapped his trademark shoulder-length hairstyle for a new short 'do, which he debuted Saturday night at his after party following his final performance.Miranda posted on his Twitter page a photo of what he leaves behind."Teach 'em how to say goodbye..." he wrote.
