Cuba Gooding Sr. Dead At 72, Suspected Drug Overdose/n

So sad.
TMZ is reporting Cuba Gooding Sr. has died.
According to the report, the father to Cuba Gooding Jr. was found dead in his car, parked on the side of Ventura Blvd in Woodland Hills, CA. Police suspect he died of a drug overdose.
He is survived by wife Shirley, their four children, and multiple grandchildren.
Gooding was the lead singer of '70s soul group The Main Ingredient, best known for the hit Everybody Plays The Fool (below).
