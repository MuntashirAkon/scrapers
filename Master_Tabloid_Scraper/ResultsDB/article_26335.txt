Veteran comedic actor Fred Willard was taken into custody for lewd conduct Wednesday night, Los Angeles police tell RadarOnline.com, after he was caught at an adult movie theater with his genitals exposed and his pants down.
The 78-year-old Ohio native was arrested at 8:45 PM PT at Tiki Theatre in Hollywood. He was later cited and released and a mug shot was taken, police said, adding he was “cooperative” with authorities in the ordeal.
