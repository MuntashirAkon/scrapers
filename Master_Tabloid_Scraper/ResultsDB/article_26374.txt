Al and Willard Talk About an Alligator/n
You never know what pearls of mindless nonsense are going to fall from the lips of Willard Scott (though he is 106 years old, so perhaps we should cut him some slack), but this time he really trots out a good one. Apparently there was an alligator on the Today Show earlier, so Willard and Al's little back-and-forth isn't as bizarre as it may seem. But still...
/n
