 Marcia Wallace Dies At 70, Her 'Simpsons' Character Edna Krabappel To Be Retired.../n
Marcia Wallace’s voice and laugh were so unique that her The Simpsons character of Edna Krabappel
is being retired as a result of the actress’ death on Friday.
Wallace, 70, who first came to fame on The Bob Newhart Show, died from pneumonia.
She’d voiced the chain-smoking teacher, Mrs. Krabappel since The Simpsons began in 1990.
