Dolce & Gabbana

Being a timeless beauty icon comes with major perks, right Sophia Loren?For the 81-year-old, those perks now include having a namesake Dolce & Gabbana lipstick shade…and the modeling job to promote the new beauty good. The lipstick, dubbed Sophia Loren N°1, is a semi-sheer lipstick in Sophia's signature shade of red (really, it's more of a muted cherry color). And as if that weren't enough, the Italian actress—who celebrated her birthday two days ago, no less—is more radiant than ever in the new ads and accompanying campaign video for the lipstick shade, reminding everyone in the biz that she's still an ageless beauty.
