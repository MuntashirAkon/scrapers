YOGA WORKS/n
TV star Constance Marie is  a first time mom at 43.
Marie gave birth  to a little girl on February 5th  in Santa Monica. The father is her fiancé, yoga-instructor Kent Katich .  
According to People, the baby girl was born at 4:12 p.m. and weighed 8 lbs., 13 oz.
