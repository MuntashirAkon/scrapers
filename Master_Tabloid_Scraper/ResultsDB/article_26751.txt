So many awards shows, so little time,

The nominees for the 21st annual Critics’ Choice Awards were announced on Monday (Dec. 14, 2015) with Mad Max: Fury Road leading the list with 13 nominations and Fargo topping the television category with 8 nominations. Jennifer Lawrence was recognized for her upcoming role in Joy and her reprisal of Katniss Everdeen in The Hunger Games: Mockingjay — Part 2.
The Critics’ Choice Awards will broadcast live on A&E, Lifetime, and LMN on Jan. 17. 2016, at 8 p.m. EST, hosted by T.J. Miller. Check to see if your favorite shows, actors, and/or films made the cut in the full nominee list below.
