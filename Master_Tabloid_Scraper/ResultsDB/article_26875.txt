Mobb Deep’s Prodigy passed away from an accident.

In his death certificate, coroners stated the late rapper passed away from chocking during his stay at a Las Vegas hospital, where he was receiving treatment for sickle cell anemia.
Via Revolt TV:
As reported by TMZ, the Clark County Medical Examiner has completed its investigation into the rapper’s death last month and listed it as accidental choking. Following his June 20 passing, it was reported that he choked on an egg while in the hospital.
