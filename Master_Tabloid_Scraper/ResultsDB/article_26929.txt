Recalling the horrific nature of Tate’s murder, the state reiterated: “Sharon Tate was stabbed 16 times. She was hung. She was pregnant. She was begging for her life.”Photo credit: MEGA3 of 10Krenwinkel dropped a bombshell about Manson’s evil control over their sex lives. “We didn’t realize that we had a pimp that was always a pimp,” She told the Board about her relationship with Manson. “When I look back at all of it…we were used no different than any prostitute he ever used before. We were given [to] men for sex.”Photo credit: MEGA4 of 10Tate was murdered when she was eight-and-a-half months pregnant, and Krenwinkel noted how she died and what she did next. “Tex had a rope and what he did is he threw it over a banister, over a piece of wood and brought it down and he started tying up Miss Tate and Miss Foster...He started tying them up and he said he was having difficulty with Miss Foster. She pulled away and she ran and he told me go after her and kill her.”
