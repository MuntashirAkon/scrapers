Patricia Arquette and Tracy Morgan Talk Favorite Summer Travel Moments/n
While some A-listers are jetting off to exotic locations like St. Tropez and Lake Como, others are spending their summer holidays close to home.From Chicago to Mississippi, stars are sharing their favorite memories from right here in the United States.For Patricia Arquette that includes Michigan. "I discovered this incredible sculpture while driving through Detroit," she says in the Global Discovery campaign from Creative Coalition and Renaissance Hotels. "It's a monument to Joe LouIs."

She's not alone. Many stars also shared that their favorite summer memories include simple art pieces."You don't have to go to a museum to see art you can see it as you walk along the path of life," Tracy Morgan says.Bon voyage!
