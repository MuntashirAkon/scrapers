Another ‘Fifty Shades’ Casting Announcement: Anastasia Steele Has a Best Friend/n
Eloise Mumford is the latest actress cast in the Fifty Shades of Grey movie. She’ll play Kate Kavanaugh, the best friend and roommate of Anastasia Steele. Mumford joins Jamie Dornan and Dakota Johnson, who’ve been cast to play the lead couple of Christian and Anastasia.
Mumford has starred in the TV shows Lone Star and The River. She was also in the little-seen, direct-t0-video Miley Cyrus/Jeremy Piven masterpiece So Undercover.
