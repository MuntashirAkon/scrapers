Terry Dubrow Slams 'Stress' Of 'RHOC' After Heather's Exit/n
Terry Dubrow is grateful his wife Heather left The Real Housewives of Orange County, RadarOnline.com has learned!
“One of the most incredible, interesting experiences of our lives was being on the Orange County Housewives, but there’s a certain component of stress that’s been removed because there’s less conflict,” the Botched star told The Daily Dish.
“She still has actually quite a relationship with some of them,” he added of her costars.
Heather, 48, appeared on five seasons of the hit Bravo show from 2012 to 2016, and quit after her explosive feud with Kelly Dodd aired during season 11. Insiders previously told Radar that she bowed out to save her marriage.
“After Heather’s marriage became a central focus last season on the show, it really caused issues with her and Terry,” a source revealed. “They both decided that that she should just quit RHOC since they both have several other successful projects and do not need the money. The last thing that either of them want is to end up in a divorce because they became reality TV stars.”
The couple tied the knot in 1999 and share four kids, Nicky, Max, Katarina and Collette.
