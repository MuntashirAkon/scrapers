The final announcement for the 2015 Teen Choice Awards has been made, as well as the hosts for this year’s show: Ludacris, Gina Rodriguez and Josh Peck.

The first wave of nominees was announced in June while the second wave was released earlier in July. Finally, the last wave of nominees has been released!
Caitlyn Jenner celebrated being nominated for Social Media Queen on Instagram, writing, “And to think I wouldn’t have qualified for this nomination a few months ago… What an honor! Link to vote in bio. #TeenChoice #SocialMediaQueen”.
