Marvel Studios has just released the second trailer for Ant-Man. 
In the new trailer, above, we see Paul Rudd in full superhero mode. We also get our first real look at some of the film’s action scenes, as well as some of the funnier ones.

Joining Rudd are Michael Douglas as Hank Pym, Corey Stoll as villainous Darren Cross/Yellowjacket, and Evangeline Lilly as Hope van Dyne. The film also features Bobby Cannavale, Michael Peña, Judy Greer, rapper T.I, and more.
Ant-Man is scheduled to be released in theaters on July 17, 2015.
