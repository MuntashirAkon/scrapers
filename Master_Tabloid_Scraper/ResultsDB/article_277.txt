Chad Michael Murray and his wife Sarah Roemer welcomed their second child together.

Murray announced the new addition to their family in a touching Instagram post on Monday, Mar. 13.
“Don’t worry little girl you can hold on as tight as you want for as long as you want,” he captioned the photo below. “I’m already yours and I’ll never let you go. I’ve now two incredible women in my life. My son and I are two lucky guys.”
