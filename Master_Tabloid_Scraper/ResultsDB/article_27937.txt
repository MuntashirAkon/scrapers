Glenne Headly passed away on Thursday night (June 8, 2017) at the age of 63.

The beloved actress was known for starring alongside Warren Beatty in Dick Tracy, as well as her roles in Dirty Rotten Scoundrels, Mr. Holland’s Opus, Monk, and The Night Of.
Via TMZ:
Glenne’s rep says, “It is with deep sorrow that we confirm the passing of Glenne Headly.” The rep did not say anything about her cause of death.
