Nick Gordon Granted $15.5K Bond After Girlfriend Accuses Him Of Brutal Beatdown/n
Nick Gordon was released on bail Sunday morning, June 11, after he was arrested for allegedly beating up a “female victim” who identified herself as his girlfriend.
His bail was set at $15,000 — plus was charged an extra $500 for the domestic battery case. The judge also ordered that Gordon have a GPS monitor slapped around his ankle, and was prohibited from going back to the couple’s house where his girlfriend would remain.
Gordon is set to appear in court on August 1.

As Radar reported, the incident occured in Sanford, Florida, where the pair allegedly got into a heated argument after returning home from a sports bar. The young female went on to accuse Gordon of pounding her with a closed fist, even striking her in the head.
