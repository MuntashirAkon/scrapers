Megyn Kelly's Controversial Alex Jones Interview A Ratings Bust/n
Megyn Kelly‘s Sunday Night has seen no growth after her scandalous interview with conspiracy theorist Alex Jones, RadarOnline.com can report.
As Radar readers know, Kelly received major attention and backlash after she announced her controversial interview with Jones, who shocked Sandy Hook Elementary survivors and their loved ones by denying the mass shooting’s existence.
Yesterday’s episode averaged 3.5 million viewers, a 0.5 rating among adults 18-49 and a 0.7 rating among adults 25-54, according to The Hollywood Reporter.
