Today (Dec. 14, 2016), Property Brothers’ Drew Scott announced that he and his girlfriend of six years Linda Phan have decided to tie the knot!

“Every day for the past 6 years my love for Linda has grown stronger & last night I became the luckiest man in the world when she said #ido,” he captioned some adorable photos of himself and his soon-to-be bride.

Linda works for Drew and his twin brother Jonathan Scott’s company Scott Brothers Entertainment handling everything from digital content to merchandise lines for the home-renovating duo.
According to People, Scott proposed to Phan by presented her with a cake which said “Oh the Places We’ll Go” in icing on top, a spin on the beloved Dr. Seuss book Oh the Places You’ll Go, a favorite of hers.
Congrats, Drew and Linda!
