'Heroes' Star Charged with DUI  /nAdrian Pasdar -- who played Senator Nathan Petrelli on  "Heroes " -- has officially been charged with DUI following his arrest last month in L.A.The 44-year-old actor -- who's married to Dixie Chicks singer Natalie Maines -- was initially pulled over on January 27 after cops say they clocked him going 90 MPH on the 405 Freeway.After conducting a field sobriety test, cops eventually arrested Pasdar and held him on $15,000 bail.Pasdar is due back in court on February 24 and faces up to six months in jail and a $1,000 fine.
