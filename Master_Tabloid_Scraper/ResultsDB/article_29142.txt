Dick Van Dyke, 86, Marries 40-Year-Old Arlene Silver/n
By Radar Staff
Dick Van Dyke secretly took a walk down the aisle February 29, the Mary Poppins star has now revealed.
The 86-year-old Van Dyke wed Arlene Silver, a 40-year-old make-up artist. The two have known each other six years.
One of the most successful TV actors of all time, Van Dyke’s first marriage ended in divorce. He then lived with Michelle Triola from 1976 until her death in 2009.
Congratulations to the newlyweds!
