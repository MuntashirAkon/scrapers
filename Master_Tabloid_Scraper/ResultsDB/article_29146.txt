Paul McCartney and Fiancee Nancy Shevell Dine Out!/n
Sir Paul McCartney and fiancee Nancy Shevell dine out with her son, Arlen in central London ahead of their wedding on Sunday. As the happy couple left Cecconi’s restaurant_ they received congratulations from a Big Issue seller. Sir Paul and Nancy will marry on October 9 2011 at Westminster Registry Office in the British Capital.
