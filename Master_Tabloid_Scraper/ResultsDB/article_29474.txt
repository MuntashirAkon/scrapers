Tyga Gonna Get Stabbed! Rapper Tapped For Season Three Of MTV's Scream!/n

Maybe this will help Tyga's money problems!
The rapper, along with C.J. Wallace (Biggie and Faith Evans' son), has been cast in a leading role for season three of MTV's Scream!
The Hollywood Reporter reports production will move from New Orleans to Atlanta, and the show will be executive produced by Queen Latifah and run by Brett Matthews.
The six-episode third season will premiere in March 2018 as a three-night event. Stay tuned!
