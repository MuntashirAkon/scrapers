Chris Brown and Lil Wayne Involved in Drug Investigation/n
Chris Brown and Lil Wayne have found themselves in the center of a federal drug investigation after music producer Harrison Garcia is said to have  bragged about selling drugs to the two rappers.

Hip Hop My Way reports:
Allegedly, Garcia sent a text message saying “I’ll shoot u some trees. It’s for Wayne.” The producer, supposedly, also sent a message reading, “Look who put money in my account,” while accompanied with a screenshot of a $15,000 bank deposit from Chris Brown. Oops!
…
Well, an FBI agent testified, saying the producer admitted to selling Wayne a lot of drugs; he’s also got pictures of the two together. It’s not looking to good for this trio.
