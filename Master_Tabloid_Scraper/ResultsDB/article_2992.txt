Picks and Pans Review: The Fannie Farmer Junior Cookbook/n
by Juan Scobey
Illustrated by Patience Brewster
Given a face-lift after 36 years, Fannie Farmer’s classic cookbook nudges novices toward basics from kitchen equipment to cooking techniques. The recipes are simple too. So for those new to slinging the pots and pans, here’s a fine way to get started. (Little, Brown, $19.95)
