Peter Parker has his plate full with extracurricular activities.

The first trailer for Spider-Man: Homecoming, which debuted on Jimmy Kimmel Live on Thursday (Dec. 8, 2016), shows that Parker (Tom Holland) is a busy high school student. He’s battling local criminals, the Vulture, and his emotions as he daydreams about his enchanting classmate (Laura Harrier) from afar.

Tony Stark (Robert Downey Jr.) makes an appearance in the trailer as well to offer Parker some superhero advice: “Don’t do anything I would do. And definitely don’t do anything I wouldn’t do. There’s a little gray area in there, and that’s where you operate.”
