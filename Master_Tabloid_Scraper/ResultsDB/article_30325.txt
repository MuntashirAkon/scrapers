Jack Tweed, the widower of late British reality star Jade Goody, was released Friday from HMP Edmunds Hill Prison after serving half of a 12-week sentence in connection with his attack on a cabbie.
He left the correctional facility with his mother, Mary, and his good friend Mark Wright.
