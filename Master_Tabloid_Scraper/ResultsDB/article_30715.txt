A teenager dropped 25 feet from a ride at Six Flags in Queensbury New York, RadarOnline.com can report.
In a shocking video from the Associated Press, the 14-year-old girl is seen hanging from the side of the car as operators halt the ride and a crowd gathers beneath her.
In a shocking moment, the girl lets go and is struck by a tree branch, before being caught by the group below.

A 47-year-old man was also taken to Glens Falls Hospital with a back injury he sustained in the group catch.
