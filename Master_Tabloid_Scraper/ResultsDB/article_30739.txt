Actor Steven Yeun – best known as The Walking Dead fan favorite Glenn Rhee, now deceased (*sob*) — wed his longtime girlfriend, Joana Pak, on Saturday (Dec. 3, 2016).
The couple tied the knot at the Paramour Estate in Los Angeles, reports E! News. Pak’s sister, Kayce, posted a sweet pic of the two on Instagram.



Getting to celebrate the love between these two, last night, along with all their friends and family was so beautiful and special!! This whole week has been an awesome, family-filled, fun time and it’s been so great! (We’ve also gained a brother in-law from it!) #loveyouguys #pakpack
A photo posted by Kayce Pak (@thepakpack) on Dec 4, 2016 at 9:34am PST



Yeun’s TWD co-stars Andrew Lincoln, Norman Reedus, Melissa McBride, Chandler Riggs, Jon Bernthal, Alanna Masterson and Sarah Wayne Callies all attended.
