Cops Sue NFL Star LeSean McCoy Over 'Serious and Permanent Injuries' From Bar Fight/nLeSean McCoy has been hit with a lawsuit by two off-duty cops who say the Buffalo Bills running back and his crew beat the hell out of them during a bar fight in Philly.  The officers behind the suit, Darnell Jessie and Roland Butler, claim they were partying at Recess Lounge back in February when McCoy and his crew attacked them.   An attorney for the officer told ESPN both men suffered  "serious and permanent injuries. " The officers are also suing Recess and 3 members of McCoy's party -- Curtis Brinkley, Tamarcus Porter and Christopher Henderson. McCoy and his party had been investigated by cops in the past, but officials declined to press charges. /n
