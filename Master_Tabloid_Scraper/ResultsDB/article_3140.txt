Stephanie Beatriz Says Working On 'Brooklyn Nine-Nine' Is 'A Dream Come True'/n
Actress Stephanie Beatriz headed to the third annual Bentonville Film Festival this week, and spoke exclusively with Radar about her role as Detective Rosa Diaz in the Fox hit comedy show Brooklyn Nine-Nine.
“It’s a really weird dream come true. I didn’t know if there was going to be a place for me on television,” she said.
