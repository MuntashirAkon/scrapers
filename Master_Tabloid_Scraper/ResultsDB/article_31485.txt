Daniel Baldwin Welcomes A New Baby Girl/n
Daniel Baldwin has had a lot on his plate lately. Days after responding to a restraining order request, the actor ushered in some good news: he and his wife Joanne Smith-Baldwin welcomed the newest addition to their family!
At 9:35 a.m. on Friday, the family welcomed baby girl Finley Rae Martineau Baldwin into the world at Cedars Sinai hospital in Los Angeles. The bouncing baby weighed in at 7 lbs. 10 oz. and was 20 1/2 inches. 
Baldwin and Joanne have another daughter Avis Ann who was born 18 months ago and is “excited to meet her new little sister.” /n
