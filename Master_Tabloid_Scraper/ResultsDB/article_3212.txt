Ex-Menudo Member Robi Draco Rosa -- Diagnosed with Cancer/nRobi Draco Rosa -- a former member of the Latin boy band Menudo -- has announced he's been diagnosed with cancer. Rosa's manager released a statement ... explaining how doctors discovered a cancerous tumor in the singer's abdomen, near his liver. The 41-year-old singer is currently receiving treatment at a clinic in Houston, TX. Rosa joined Menudo in 1984 and stuck with group until '87.
