Michael Nyqvist Dead — Killed By Lung Cancer At 56/n
Michael Nyqvist died on Tuesday after a horrifying battle with lung cancer, RadarOnline.com has learned.
The actor, who starred in the Swedish version of Girl With the Dragon Tattoo, was just 56 when he passed, according to reports.
“Michael’s joy and passion were infectious to those who knew and loved him,” his rep said in a statement to the Daily News.
