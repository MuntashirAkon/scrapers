Annie Leibovitz Portfolio Safe -- For Now/nFamed photog Annie Leibovitz has managed to stave off losing her entire legendary portfolio -- but there's still the pesky matter of the $24 million she owes. Leibovitz got so deep into financial debt, she took out the loan last year and put up her homes -- and her entire photo library -- as collateral.Art Capital Group has agreed to withdraw its lawsuit against Leibovitz for failing to pay back her loan on time.
