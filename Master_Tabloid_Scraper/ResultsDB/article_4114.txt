‘Nashville’ Season 5 Trailer Confirms Hayden Panettiere’s Return — Watch the Dramatic Teaser!/n
She’s alive! A new promo for season 5 of Nashville confirms Hayden Panettiere’s return to the country drama, which will premiere on its new network, CMT, this January.

In the dramatic teaser, which also includes footage of stars Connie Britton (Rayna James) and Charles Esten (Deacon Claybourne), Panettiere’s character Juliette says, “We tape ourselves together so that no one can see our flawed hearts,” as footage from last season is shown of her breaking down and crying.
