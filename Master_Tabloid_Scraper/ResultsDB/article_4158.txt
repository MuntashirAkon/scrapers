 Major award shows usually don’t release a pre-nomination list, but this is the Razzies, so they can pretty much do anything they want to.
The “anti-Oscars” award show has released their official ballots with the pre-nominations of who is eligible to win (or lose, I guess).

Selena Gomez is up for her film Getaway, co-starring Ethan Hawke. While Liam Hemsworth picked up worst actor nominations for Paranoia and Love and Honor.
Kim Kardashian is up for a Worst Supporting Actress nomination in Tyler Perry’s Temptation.
The Razzie winners will be announced the night before the Academy Awards on March 1, 2014.
