Watch Sean 'Puff Daddy' Combs On WWE's 'Monday Night Raw'/n
Sean “Puff Daddy” Combs appeared on last night’s episode of Monday Night Raw on USA Network, and RadarOnline.com has all the details!
No stranger to the WWE Universe, Puff Daddy had previously performed a memorable set at WrestleMania 29 in 2013 .
For his return to the network, the famed rapper chatted with Mick Foley and The New Day, and proceeded to dig into a box of their signature cereal that had been gifted to him during the segment.
Earlier this year, Combs appeared in a special, one-night only Bad Boy Reunion concert at Brooklyn’s Barclays Center. The show included appearances by Diddy, Faith Evans, Lil’ Kim, Ma$e, French Montana, Jay Z and Mary J. Blige. /n
