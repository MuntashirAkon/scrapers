Winona Ryder: I Get Held Up at Airport Security Because of ‘Beetlejuice’/n
Twenty-seven years after Michael Keaton first wreaked havoc on the Deetzes in Tim Burton’s 1988 comedy, Winona Ryder is gearing up to reprise her role as Lydia Deetz in an upcoming sequel.

Appearing on Late Night With Seth Meyers on Monday, Aug. 10, the Heathers actress dished what she knows about the Beetlejuice sequel — and why she sometimes gets held up at TSA because of it.
