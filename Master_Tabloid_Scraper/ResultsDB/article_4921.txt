Wedding bells are ringing for Chris Hardwick and Lydia Hearst!The comedian and his longtime girlfriend tied the knot on Saturday evening in front of their closest family and friends, a rep for the actor confirmed to E! News.Close to 450 guests watched the couple exchange vows during a Walking Dead-themed wedding in Pasadena, Calif. The bride wore a custom-designed Christian Siriano gown while Hardwick donned a custom Prada suit. Ahead of their nuptials, Hardwick couldn't contain his excitement, posting an adorable photo of the pair sharing a sweet smooch during their wedding rehearsals. A snapshot shared by Hearst confirmed the two would wed on August 20, complete with an H-squared graphic preceded by a cartoon drawing of the couple as zombies.After all, Chris isn't known by his fans as the Nerdist for nothing, right?!Chris and Lydia's special day wouldn't be complete without a philanthropic twist, as the couple opted to forgo the traditional wedding registry and instead asked their guests to donate to a number of charities.
