Officials announced that a “thorough investigation” is currently taking place. The other contestant has not been named because she may be a victim of alleged sexual misconduct.4 of 13JoJo Fletcher’s runner-up Robby Hayes was arrested for a DUI in 2012 when he was pulled over by police for crossing the double solid lines of the road. “I observed the defendant to have bloodshot and watery eyes,” the police report read. “I smelled a moderate odor of an alcoholic beverage coming from the defendant as he spoke to me. The defendant’s speech was slurred and talked with a thick tongue.” Hayes refused to answer questions and denied drinking alcohol, claiming he was the “designated driver.” Hayes also refused to take the Standardized Field Sobriety Exercises and was arrested. He pled not guilty and the charge was dismissed in September 2013.Photo credit: Getty Images
