Prince's Drummer Dead At 43 Following Cancer Battle/n
John Blackwell Jr., who served as Prince‘s drummer for 15 years, has died following a battle with cancer. He was just 43.
The musician’s wife, Yaritza, announced the tragic news on Instagram Tuesday, sharing a photo of his hand in hers at the hospital.
“My husband incredible drummer John Blackwell Jr. passed the way [sic] peacefully in my company today,” she wrote alongside the image. “Thanks God for his life and thanks everyone for their support.”

According to reports, Blackwell, who lived in Florida, was diagnosed with brain tumors last year.
In the summer of 2016, he went to Tampa General Hospital’s Rehabilitation Center to learn how to walk again after the tumors affected the use of his left arm and leg.
