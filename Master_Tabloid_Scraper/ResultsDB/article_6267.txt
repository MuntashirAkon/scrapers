Kylie Jenner is more than just her signature pout.

Though the reality star may be more known for her sold out lip kits these days, she also knows a thing or two then it comes to eye makeup. In a new beauty tutorial shared on her app, the 18-year-old breaks down the easiest way to get a smoky eye.
According to her, you can use just three products to achieve the glamorous look — like the one she rocked while attending her mother’s The Great Gatsby-themed birthday party!
“I love how the final look put a fresh twist on recreating a classic 1920s dramatic vibe,” she writes. “I love the versatility of this look! The pink and purple tones make it work for both daytime and nighttime.”
Here’s the step-by-step guide.
 
Step 1: Base Color
Jenner’s makeup artist Ariel Tejada first applied Chanel Illusion D’Ombre Long Wear Luminous Eyeshadow in Emerveille across the eyelid and up to the crease.
