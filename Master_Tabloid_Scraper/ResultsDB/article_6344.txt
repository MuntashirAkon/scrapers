When it came to her bridal look, the new Mrs. Derek Jeter knocked it out of the park.Model Hannah Jeter, 26, married the 42-year-old retired New York Yankees star Saturday in front of family and friends in an outdoor ceremony at the Meadowood Napa Valley Resort in St. Helena, California.She walked down the aisle in a custom Vera Wang mermaid bridal gown that featured a bodice with a sweetheart neckline, tulle and lace halter high neck and plunging back, and tulle and hand-appliqued Chantilly lace-covered skirt. She paired the look with a double-tier cathedral-length veil over an updo and carried a large bouquet of white flowers.

The groom wore a black and white Nigel Curtiss tux with an ivory bow tie and and a white rose boutonniere. Davis' bridesmaids sported one-shoulder, lavender, full-length chiffon dresses and carried small white flower bouquets.

The bride later changed into a custom Vera Wang V-neck halter gown with tulle detail and a silk georgette A-line skirt.There reception took place inside an open greenhouse.
