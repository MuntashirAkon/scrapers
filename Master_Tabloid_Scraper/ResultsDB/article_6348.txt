Radar Movie Preview: Wild River/n
When U.S. Wildlife Service Agent Cory Lambert (Jeremy Renner) discovers a dead body on the Wind River Indian Reservation, the FBI sends rookie agent Jane Banner (Elizabeth Olsen) to investigate. It soon becomes clear that she’s unprepared – and not just for the unforgiving Wyoming winter. Together, Cory and Jane infiltrate and community ravaged by drugs to hunt down a predator in this award-winning whodunit.
