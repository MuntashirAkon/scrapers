Harry Styles' Stepfather, Robin Twist, Dead/n
Harry Styles’ stepfather has died, RadarOnline.com has learned exclusively.
Sources close to the family tell us that Robin Twist passed away on Tuesday night. The cause of death is unknown at this time.
Twist wed Styles’ mother, Anne Cox, June 2013 in Congleton in Cheshire. The singer was the best man at the wedding and even walked his mother down the aisle.
