Photo credit: Instagram/@hopps12714Nance was an aspiring musician based in Houston, Texas. Before appearing on Emily's season of the Bachelorette, he had been giving guitar lessons to the blind. He has a personal youtube channel dedicated to his music. Nance studied biomedical science at Texas A&M University, graduating in 2008. He went on to work as a rehab counselor where he counseled men in recovery from drug and alcohol addiction, according to his Linkedin.
