Diddy's Screening of 'Can't Stop, Won't Stop' Draws Madonna, Snoop and Vin/nA slew of celebs showed up at Diddy's L.A. mansion for a screening of his new flick, 'Can't Stop, Won't Stop,' and to say the least they were inspired.
