Newport Harbor: The Real Orange County star Clay Alder committed suicide at only 27 years old – and his family is heartbroken over the sudden loss. Alder’s father exclusively revealed to RadarOnline.com how the family is handling the shocking death.
“The family is grieving and we are asking everyone to respect our privacy,” Frank Adler told Radar. “He’s deceased and that’s all I can say.”
Frank explained how a memorial has been done in his late son’s honor.
