'Ghostbusters II' Star Commits Suicide By Hanging/n
Henry ‘Hank’ Deutschendorf tragically died last week at the young age of 28, RadarOnline.com can reveal.
The Ghostbusters II actor hung himself at his home in Escondido, CA, according to the Medical Examiner report. The star was found by his twin brother, William, who played Baby Oscar alongside him in the 1989 flick.
According to sources, Henry battled with schizoaffective disorder. William says Henry “fought for his life every day.”
