For more than five decades, Bill Cosby warmed the hearts of fans nationwide through his comedy shows, his hit television series The Cosby Show and his notable charitable works.
Now, it’s time to take a look deep inside the secret life of America’s once favorite dad in the hit new show, Bill Cosby: An American Scandal.
With exclusive interviews and insider details, Investigation Discovery uncovers shocking family drama and examines the allegations that brought about the comedian’s epic fall from grace.

“Dr. Cliff Huxtable — a doting father living a perfect, suburban life — appears to be a far cry from the picture painted by Bill Cosby’s accusers,” said Henry Schleiff, Group President of Investigation Discovery, Destination America and American Heroes Channel. “Between the upcoming trial and the inevitable verdict in the court of public opinion, our viewers and, indeed, all of his fans, want to know if we adored a man who was secretly a monster.”
Bill Cosby: An American Scandal premieres Monday on Investigation Discovery at 9pm ET.
