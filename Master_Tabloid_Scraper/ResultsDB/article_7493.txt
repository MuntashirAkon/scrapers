Janice Dickinson was looking to get in touch with an old friend.
In a sneak peek at Wednesday’s episode of Hollywood Medium with Tyler Henry, the former supermodel and America’s Next Top Model judge attends a reading with Henry where he seems to channel her good friend and late Saturday Night Live star John Belushi.
“I have chills,” Dickinson, 62, tells Henry in the clip after he alludes to the fact she was away when Belushi died. “I have chills on my chills.”
