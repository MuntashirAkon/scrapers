Caitlyn Jenner just made her return to the 2016 ESPY Awards!One year after receiving the Arthur Ashe Courage Award, the I Am Cait star returned to the show in a stunning white off the shoulder dress with floor-length sleeves. She completed her super-chic outfit with silver heels and a diamond bracelet.Prior to hitting the carpet, Kim Kardashian shared a few Snapchats which showed Cait trying on three different gowns in the same style. "Alright guys, we're at Caitlyn's getting her ready for the ESPYs with none other than Michael Costello and he's sewing up my top," Kim said in one Snapchat video with the designer.Costello also posted a pic with Cait and Kim on Instagram with the caption, "Was so fun helping @caitlynjenner getting ready for the @espys today along side with my friend @kimkardashian glam @robscheppy . #espys #caitlynjenner #kimkardashian."After helping Caitlyn get ready for the show, Kim did a live-stream where she said that she approves of Caitlyn's overall ESPYs look, but she just isn't into her shoes.
