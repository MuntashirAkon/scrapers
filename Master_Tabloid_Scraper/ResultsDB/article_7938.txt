Jazz Jennings -- I May Meet My Transgender Hero Caitlyn at the ESPYs! (VIDEO)/nJazz Jennings, one of the transgender community’s youngest crusaders, is a massive Caitlyn Jenner fan and she's gunning to meet her idol tonight at the ESPYs.  Our photog caught up with the 14-year-old Tuesday in Bev Hills. She's in town promoting her new reality show “I Am Jazz,” but the highlight of the trip ... she scored a ticket to the show, where Caitlyn will receive the Arthur Ashe Courage Award.  Jennings was born a male but started her transition at age 3.
