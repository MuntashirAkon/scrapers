"Green Acres" Star Albert Dead/n
     Eddie Albert, best known as the charmingly perplexed gentleman farmer of the sitcom Green Acres, has died.           The actor and environmental activist with the easy-going smile, who had suffered with Alzheimer's disease in recent years, succumbed to pneumonia Thursday at his Pacific Palisades home. He was 99.          "He died so beautifully and so gracefully that literally this morning I don't feel grief, I don't feel loss," said his actor son, Edward Albert/n
