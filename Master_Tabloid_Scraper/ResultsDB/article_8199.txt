Jenelle Evans Reveals Her Wedding Date/n
Wedding bells are in the future for Jenelle Evans!

The Teen Mom 2 star recently announced her wedding date with fiancé David Eason. According to the 25-year-old, who got engaged in February, her nuptials will take place sooner than you’d think.
Via Teen Mom Talk Now:
In a beautiful photo made by Jenelle herself, she announced that her big wedding will be taking place on September 23rd of this year. Even if it doesn’t take place during the regular filming season of Teen Mom 2, it’s likely that Jenelle will allow her wedding to be filmed.
