Charlie Hunnam, Robert Pattinson and Tom Holland Embark on a Dangerous Adventure in the Lost City of Z Teaser/n
Despite much doubt from colleagues, Fawcett is determined to return to the Amazon and find the city.He seeks support from his wife Nina Fawcett (played by Sienna Miller), his son, Jack Fawcett (Holland) and aide de camp, Henry Costin (Pattinson).

In the teaser, we watch as the determined explorers take on natives from the region, battle against the odds of nature and overcome fear and doubt to pursue their passion.The Lost City of Z will hit theaters April 14, 2017.
