From his gut-wrenching suicide note to her callous messages, the state tried to show Carter was a “reckless” killer while her attorney argued that she wasn’t responsible for Roy’s death.Photo credit: Getty Images2 of 6Roy’s father was arrested for assault and battery in February 2014 after attacking his teenage son. Mattapoisett police officer Dennis James Tavares testified during the trial that Conrad was the victim, and photographs of his injuries were entered into evidence. The defense was presented evidence of the incident assault arrest to try and show that Roy’s depression and eventual suicide stemmed from his violent relationship with his father.3 of 6Carter, then 17, was accused of sending thousands of text messages to Roy, 18, that encouraged him to kill himself. Like I don’t get why you aren’t.”Photo credit: Getty Images4 of 6Roy left a suicide note for Carter before he killed himself in his truck in a Kmart parking lot in July 2014.
