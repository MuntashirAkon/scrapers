Kim and Kourtney Kardashian spared no expense for their daughters’ joint birthday party this weekend.

North West and and Penelope Disick rang in their respective birthdays on Saturday (June 24, 2017) with a Moana themed bash. The cousins, who previously received matching Pomeranian puppies, celebrated with close friends and family at a backyard bash.
The celebrations included rainbow-colored shaved ice treats, barbecue food, a surfing machine, a petting zoo, and even impersonators of characters from the Disney film. The girls also enjoyed desserts from Hansen’s Cakes with their cousin, Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna’s daughter Dream Kardashian.
