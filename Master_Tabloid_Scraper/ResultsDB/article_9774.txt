Burglar Bunch Suspects -- We Didn't Do It!/nThe Hollywood Hills Burglar Bunch are really just a bunch of innocent kids ... or at least kids who have now officially pled not guilty.Nick Prugo was in court today and pled not guilty to eight felony burglaries.Diana Tamayo -- not guilty to two felony burglaries. Courtney Ames and Roy Lopez Jr. -- not guilty to one felony burglary.Bail for Prugo and Tamayo was set at $20,000 -- for Lopez and Ames it was $50,000.The gang will have a courtroom reunion in January./n
