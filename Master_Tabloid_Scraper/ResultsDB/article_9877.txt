Neighbor Of Murdered Stockdale Family: 'I thought It Was Target Practice!'/n
A neighbor of the Stockdale family has re-lived the shocking moment Jacob Stockdale, 25, allegedly blasted his brother James Stockdale, 21, and mother Kathryn Stockdale, 54, to death with a shotgun.
Harry Mcnutt Jr. – who lives next door to the Stockton home in Beach City, Ohio – heard the carnage at 4.30 p.m. on Thursday, June 15.
He said: “It was a rapid, you know, boom, boom, boom, boom, boom, boom, then time enough to reload, and another five or six shots so I thought it was target practice.”

Police rushed to the scene after receiving a 911 hang-up call where they discovered Jacob with an apparent self-inflicted gunshot wound to the head. His mother, Kathryn and brother James were found dead.
“This is a tragedy for the family. It is a tragedy for the community,” Stark County Sheriff George T. Maier said.
It is understood that Jacob is being treated at the MetroHealth hospital in Cleveland were he is currently in a “serious condition.”
