Zooey Deschanel Welcomes a Baby Boy with Jacob Pechenik/n
Zooey Deschanel’s family just got a little bigger!

The New Girl star welcomed her second child — a boy — with husband Jacob Pechenik last week in Los Angeles, her rep confirms. According to People, the couple named their new son Charlie Wolf Pechenik.
“Zooey, Jacob and their daughter Elsie are overjoyed to welcome the newest member of their family,” a spokesperson for the actress tells the publication.
Deschanel and Pechenik, who married in 2015, are already proud parents to one-year-old daughter Elsie Otter.
