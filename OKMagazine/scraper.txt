<p><!--more--></p>
<p>The singer — who recently released “Look What You Made Me Do,” a single that seemingly <a href="http://www.celebuzz.com/g/look-what-you-made-me-do-kim-kardashian-wages-war-against-taylor-swift/" target="_blank">takes aim at the rapper</a> and his wife, <a href="http://celebuzz.com/news/kim-kardashian" target="_blank"><strong>Kim Kardashian</strong></a> — will release her sixth studio album on a date that hits close to her nemesis’ heart. As fans have pointed out, November 10 holds a significant meaning in the Kardashian-West family as it marks the anniversary of the death of Kanye’s mother.</p>

<blockquote><p>While this could be just a coincidence, it’s hard for fans to overlook, considering how hard the rapper took his mother’s passing.</p>
<p>After all, he was raised by her as a single mother and the two were quite close. In fact, just last year, he had a breakdown that coincided with the death anniversary.</p></blockquote>
