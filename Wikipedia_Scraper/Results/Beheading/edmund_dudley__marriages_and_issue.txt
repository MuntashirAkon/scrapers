Edmund Dudley married twice:
Firstly in about 1494 to Anne Windsor, sister of Andrew Windsor, 1st Baron Windsor, by whom he had a daughter:

Elizabeth Dudley (born c.1500), who married William Stourton, 7th Baron Stourton.[5]


Elizabeth Dudley (born c.1500), who married William Stourton, 7th Baron Stourton.[5]
Secondly, between 1500 and 1503,[5] to Elizabeth Grey (c. 1480–1525), a daughter of Edward Grey, 1st Viscount Lisle (d.1492). Three sons were born to this marriage:

John Dudley, 1st Duke of Northumberland (1504[5]–22 August 1553)
Andrew Dudley (c. 1507–1559[6])
Jerome Dudley (d. after 1555), destined for the Church by his father, yet was mentally or physically incapacitated.[1]
