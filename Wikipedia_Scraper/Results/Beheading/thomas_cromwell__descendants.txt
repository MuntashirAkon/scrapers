Thomas Cromwell's son Gregory Cromwell, 1st Baron Cromwell, married Elizabeth Seymour, the sister of Queen Jane Seymour and widow of Sir Anthony Ughtred. They had five children:[73][74]
Henry Cromwell, 2nd Baron Cromwell
Edward Cromwell
Katherine Cromwell
Frances Cromwell
Thomas Cromwell
Cromwell's illegitimate daughter Jane had a daughter with her husband William Hough, Alice.[75]
Puritan leader Oliver Cromwell was the great-grandson of Richard Cromwell, Thomas Cromwell's nephew.[76]
Actor Danny Dyer discovered that he was the fifteen times great-grandson of Cromwell in the BBC series Who Do You Think You Are?
