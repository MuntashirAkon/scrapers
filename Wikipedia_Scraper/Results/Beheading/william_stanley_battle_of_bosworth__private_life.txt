Born at Lytham, Lancashire, Sir William was the younger son of Thomas Stanley, 1st Baron Stanley.[2] In 1465 he married Joan, daughter of John Beaumont, 1st Viscount Beaumont, and widow of John, Lord Lovel. He married secondly, c.1471, Elizabeth Hopton, daughter of Thomas Hopton with whom he had a daughter, Jane Stanley. They lived at Holt, Wrexham.[3]
