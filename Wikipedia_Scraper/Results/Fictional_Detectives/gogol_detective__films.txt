In 2013, a Bengali Film was made on the basis of Gogol's story Sonali Parer Rahashya which was released as Goyenda Gogol. This movie was directed by Arindam Dey.[3][4]
In 2014 another film Gogoler Kirti was released. It was directed by Pompy Ghosh Mukherjee based on the story Gogoler Royraja Uddhar and Mahishmardini Uddhar. In both film the character of Gogol was featured by child actor Ahijit Ghosh.[5]
