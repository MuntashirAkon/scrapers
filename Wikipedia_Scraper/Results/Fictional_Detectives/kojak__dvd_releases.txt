Universal Studios Home Entertainment released season 1 of Kojak on DVD in Regions 1, 2 and 4 in 2005–2006. Due to poor sales, no further seasons were released. This release has been discontinued and is now out of print.
On February 14, 2017, Universal Studios re-released season 1 on DVD in Region 1.[14]
On May 25, 2011, it was announced that Shout! Factory had acquired the Region 1 DVD rights to the series.[15] They have subsequently released the remaining 4 seasons on DVD as well as a complete movie collection.[16]
In Region 2, Mediumrare Entertainment has released seasons 2-5 on DVD in the UK.
In Region 4, Shock Entertainment has released seasons 2–5 on DVD in Australia.[17]
