In 1974, Barbara Mullen played Miss Marple in Murder at the Vicarage at the Savoy Theatre, London.
In September 1977, veteran actress and author Dulcie Gray played the Miss Marple character in a stage adaptation of A Murder Is Announced at the Vaudeville Theatre in London, England[14] that also featured Dinah Sheridan, Eleanor Summerfield, Patricia Brake and Barbara Flynn.[15]
