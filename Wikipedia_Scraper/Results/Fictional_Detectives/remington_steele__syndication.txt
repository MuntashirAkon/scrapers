After a nearly two decade absence from local syndication, the series returned to broadcast TV and was seen from September 3 to December 31, 2012 on MeTV, and has resumed for September 2013. It is also currently airing on Family Net. Reruns have previously aired on A&E from 1995 to 1997 and again on PAX (now Ion Television) from 2000 to 2001. It has also aired in India on Star Plus in early 1990s to March 31, 1993.
MeTV also ran the series starting Memorial Day 2017, in place of Diagnosis Murder, beginning with the first episode of the second season. But effective 8/21/2017, Diagnosis Murder returns, picking up from where they left off with season 8 episode 7: "Hot House".
