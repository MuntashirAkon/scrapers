Kardashian was born on October 21, 1980 in Los Angeles, California, to parents Robert and Kris (née Houghton).[12] She has an older sister Kourtney, a younger sister Khloé, and a younger brother Rob.[13] Their mother is of Dutch, English, Irish and Scottish ancestry,[14] while their father was a third-generation Armenian American.[15] After their parents divorced in 1991, her mother married again that year, to Caitlyn Jenner (then Bruce[A]), the 1976 Summer Olympics decathlon winner.[16] Through their marriage, Kardashian gained step-brothers Burton "Burt", Brandon, and Brody; step-sister Casey; and half-sisters Kendall and Kylie Jenner.[17]
As a teenager, she attended Marymount High School, a Roman Catholic all-girls school in Los Angeles.[18] In 1994, her father represented football player O. J. Simpson during his murder trial. Simpson is Kim Kardashian's godfather.[19] Kim's father died in 2003 of cancer.[20] In her 20s, Kardashian was the close friend and stylist of socialite Paris Hilton, through whom she first garnered media attention.[2][21]
